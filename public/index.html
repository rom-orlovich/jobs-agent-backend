      Sivan Tehila

      Cybersecurity Entrepreneur | CEO @ Onyxia | Cybersecurity Program Director & Professor @ Yeshiva University | Execution is Power 🎯

      We're looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our team to lead, innovate, and play an active role in developing our application roadmap


      Your Role

      We are seeking an exceptional Backend Engineer who wants to be part of bringing innovation and creativity to the cybersecurity world. You are motivated in delivering well documented, clean, and efficient code with a passion and curiosity for the art of programming. You enjoy living by the code of your craft and developing mind-blowing solutions for complex problems.


      As one of the first key hires to grow our teaching team, you will report directly to the Solution Architect and have influence and real impact in an early stage start-up on the expansion of our engineering team to build products and features that accelerate our traction.


      You enjoy collaborating with different Team members and can turn ideas into tangible solutions but can also work independently with speed and efficiency.


      Responsibilities
      • Build a multi-tenant SaaS cyber security solution
      • Designing and coding the data warehouse to desired company specifications
      • Working with the data analytics team to integrate with SaaS security vendors
      • Extracting data from various sources and transferring it into the warehouse for further analysis
      • Developing automated tests, and deployment processes
      • Plan and estimate time efforts and work with team and Solution Architect
      • Make a direct and meaningful contribution to the product
      • Provide status updates during daily stand-up meetings


      Experience
      • Bachelor’s degree in computer science, information technology, or a related field
      • 6+ years of related professional experience in a structured, process-driven environment
      • 6+ years of experience writing backend solutions using Python 
      • 5+ years of experience working with RDB databases such as PostgreSQL or MySQL
      • 5+ years of experience with Asynchronous Programming (async/await)
      • 5+ years of experience engineering RESTful API’s and integrating with 3rd-Party API vendors.
      • 5+ years of experience in GIT, know how to branch and rebase
      • Experience in developing serverless solutions using AWS lambda, Event Bridge, SQS, SNS.
      • Ability to troubleshoot and solve complex technical problems
      • Good verbal and written communication in English.
      • Strong sense of ownership and accountability.
      • Excellent autonomous problem-solving ability.
      Nice to have
      • Experience in working with startups, understanding their dynamics and problems
      • Experience with Docker and containerization
      • Experience developing SaaS products
      • Familiarity with Amazon Web Services (AWS)
